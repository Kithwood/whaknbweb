The Art of Tang Soo Do Mi Guk Kwan

At the West Haven Academy of Karate in New Braunfels, Texas you will be taught the Mi Guk Kwan style of the classical Korean martial art of Tang Soo Do. The Mi Guk Kwan, founded by the legendary Kwan Jhang Nim Charles Ferraro, offers students a well rounded, complete curriculum that will challenge the mind, body, and spirit of each and every student dedicated to achieving excellence in all areas of life.
